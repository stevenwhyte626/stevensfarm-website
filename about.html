Our Story: From Humble Roots to Industry Leaders

The legacy

Founded in 2005, Steven'sFarm started with a simple goal: to provide fresh, healthy, and high -quality poultry to our community(Dormaa-Ahenkro). Our farm emphasizes ethical farming practices, natural feed, and a clean, safe environment for our birds.

Today, while our farm has expanded and modernized, our core values remain unchanged. We are committed to ethical animal husbandry, sustainable practices, and the unwavering belief that happier chickens produce better products.

Our Mission

To deliver nutritious and fresh poultry products while promoting sustainable farming.

Our Vision

To be recognized as the most trusted poultry farm in Dormaa Ahenkro and Ghana, known for quality, transparency, and care for animals.

Our Milestones

  1. 2005: The Whyte family starts Steven'sFarm with a focus on free-range eggs.
  2. 2008: First expansion of the farm and establishment of the Ethical Farming Protocol.
  3. 2010: Achieved "Certified Humane" status, recognizing our exceptional animal welfare practices.
  4. 2018: Launched our state-of-the-art, low-impact processing facility to ensure local control over freshness.
  5. 2023: Introduced our full line of marinated, ready-to-cook chicken products.
