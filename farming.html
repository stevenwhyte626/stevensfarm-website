Our Ethical Farming Standards

We are dedicated to animal welfare, sustainability, and producing the healthiest products possible.

Pillars of Our Protocol

Our farming goes beyond basic requirements. We employ a Certified Humane protocol that ensures our birds live their lives as naturally and comfortably as possible. This commitment is the foundation of our product quality.

  • Open-Air Roaming: Chickens have continuous daytime access to spacious, grassy pastures.
  • Natural Diet: 100% vegetarian, non-GMO feed, enriched with vitamins and minerals.
  • No Antibiotics or Hormones: Ever. We rely on pristine living conditions to keep our flock healthy.
  • Low-Stress Handling: Training and facilities are designed to minimize stress during collection and processing.

We believe that when we respect the animals and the land, the superior quality is undeniable.

Commitment to the Environment

Sustainability isn't just a trend; it's essential for our future. Our farm integrates several environmentally friendly practices:

Water Conservation

We use rainwater harvesting systems and efficient drip feeders to significantly reduce water usage.

Solar Power

A portion of our energy needs is met by on-site solar panels, reducing our carbon footprint.

Waste Management

Manure is composted and used as natural fertilizer, creating a closed-loop system for the farm.
