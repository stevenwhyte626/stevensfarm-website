Our Range of Farm-Fresh Products

From eggs laid this morning to ethically-raised whole chickens, find the perfect product for your table.

1. Farm Fresh Eggs

Our eggs are laid by happy, free-roaming hens, resulting in deep golden yolks and rich flavor.

View Egg Specifications

We offer eggs in various sizes (Large, Jumbo) and colors (Brown, White). All are packed in recyclable cartons.

  • Brown Eggs: Rich in Omega-3 fatty acids.
  • Quail Eggs: Specialty item, available seasonally.

2. Processed & Ready-to-Cook Poultry

Expertly prepared chicken, meeting the highest hygiene standards and ready for your kitchen.

View Processing Details

Our processing uses a rapid chill system to lock in freshness without chemical preservatives. We ensure minimal handling and a fully traceable supply chain.

  • Whole Chickens (Dressed)
  • Boneless Breasts and Thighs
  • Marinated Grilling Cuts

3. Live Birds (For Commercial Buyers)

We supply healthy, vaccinated day-old chicks and mature birds to other farms and commercial buyers.

View Live Bird Purchase Requirements

Minimum order quantity is 100 units. Requires valid farm accreditation and completion of our buyer registration form.
